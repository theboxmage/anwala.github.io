In Pictures: 2018 Winter Olympics opening ceremony
The 2018 Winter Olympics opening ceremony kicked off the 23rd Games in Pyeongchang, South Korea, on Feb 9, 2018. Nearly 3,000 athletes from 92 countries are taking part this year.
