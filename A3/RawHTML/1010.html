Show Your Support for LGBTQ Olympians!

A record 12 openly LGBTQ athletes will be competing at the 2018 Winter Olympics in PyeongChang, South Korea. It is vital for LGBTQ people to be represented on the international stage, especially in sports. Help us send a message of support and encouragement to these brave Olympians to let them know that we’re cheering for them.

Send your custom message using the form below. We’ll continue to collect them throughout the 2018 Winter Olympics and share our favorites across social media.

Head to Advocate.com for info on this year’s openly LGBTQ Olympians and more illustrations by Ego Rodriguez.

Send a message of gratitude to cheer on the 2018 LGBTQ Olympians.


GLAAD rewrites the script for LGBTQ acceptance. As a dynamic media force, GLAAD tackles tough issues to shape the narrative and provoke dialogue that leads to cultural change.
