By: Maggie Hendricks | February 9, 2018 7:25 am

Tonga’s Pita Taufatofua made a name for himself at the Rio Olympics when he marched shirtless into the arena before competing in judo. Not only is he back for the Winter Olympics as a cross country skier, he again was shirtless — and this time in freezing temperatures that dipped into the 20s.

He said ahead of time he wouldn’t be shirtless:

“I want to still be alive for my race. It’s going to be freezing, so I will be keeping nice and warm,” he said.

But something made him change his mind, as he led the Tonga delegation wearing that same national outfit as he wore in Rio de Janeiro to slightly warmer temperatures.
