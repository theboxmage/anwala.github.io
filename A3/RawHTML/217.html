Don't want to miss a second of Olympic biathlon? We have you covered.

With expectations of earning its first Olympic medal at the 2018 PyeongChang Olympics, U.S. Biathlon has its first opportunity Saturday in the women’s 7.5km sprint.

Five Americans will be participating in the race, with Susan Dunklee and Clare Egan being the ones to watch.

The women’s 7.5km sprint kicks things off Saturday at 6:15 a.m. EST. You can stream it live right here.
