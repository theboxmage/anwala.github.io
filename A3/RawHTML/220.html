Published on Aug 24, 2012

The London 2012 Olympic Games were not only filled with competition, triumphs and medals, but family as well. Olympic sponsor, P&G, realized the need for the athletes to have a home away from home throughout their stay in London. More from Pampers: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZEYOT...

The P&G Family Home offered the athletes beauty treatment stations, laundry services, meals, internet access and even a playground sponsored by Pampers. Sesame Street's Elmo, Abby and Grover even stopped by the Pampers Playground to celebrate the Olympic Games and its unique spirit of play.
