Nigeria Athletes Team Wears Traditional “Gele” Attire at the Winter Olympics 2018

February 10, 2018 Chee Powers Leave a comment

Today is the kick-off of the 2018 Winter Olympics kicked in PyeongChang, South Korea, with several countries participating.  The Nigerian team rocked the stage with a GREEN/WHITE/GREEN synonymous with Nigeria… topped with a green gele and black mid-calf boots.

The represntatives of the Nigerian team consists of Ngozi Onwumere, Akuoma Omeoga, Seun Adigun and Simi Adeagbo. They will be representing Nigeria in the Bobsled and Skeleton respectively. They will be the first team to ever qualify for the winter Olympics from Africa.
